ENGINEERING ENTRANCE EXAMS IN INDIA

First step for Admission to College is Appearing for Entrance Exam

Engineering is one of the most popular career choice of graduation among the student community of India. First step towards the admission to these engineering colleges is filling application form and appearing for Entrance Exam or Competitive Exams. Every year lakhs of students appear for these Entrance Exams in India.

Every year many entrance exams are conducted at National level as well as State level for short listing candidates for the admission to colleges across the Nation. The one which attracts the Engineering students the most i.e. Joint Entrance Exam (JEE) which is a gateway to the esteemed IIT's and NIT's and many other Centrally Funded Institute of India.

However, the seats are limited and cracking JEE-advance in definitely not a cakewalk. Apart from from JEE, there are many other Engineering Exams for which you can appear to fulfil your dream. So if you don't clear the JEE than consider other Engineering Entrance Exams options that you think that you can take.
