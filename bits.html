Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani

Birla Institute of Technology and Science has campus in Pilani, Goa, Hyderabad and Dubai

The Birla Institute of Technology & Science, BITS Pilani is an all-India Institute for higher education. The institute is a dream come true of its founder late Mr G.D.Birla - an eminent industrialist, a participant in Indian freedom struggle and a close associate of the Father of Indian Nation late Mr. Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi (Mahatma Gandhi). What started in early 1900s as a small school.

The first step for the admission to BITS college at any of the Pilani campus, Hyderabad campus and Goa campus is appearing for BITSAT, a fully online exam. BITSAT tests students on their aptitude for Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry. BITSAT also has a section on English and Logical reasoning, thus becoming a complete analysis of a candidateâ€™s intelligence and capability.

BITS Pilani offers degree programmes in Engineering, Sciences, Technology, Pharmacy, Management and Humanities at Pilani campus, Goa Campus and Hyderabad Campus.

Cut-off for different Branches/Majors of BITS Pilani at different campus depends on various factors factors- Number of appliation received, Number of applicants appeared, Availability of seats on corresponding campus, test complexity level, pass proportion of the applicants. Based on above factors they will decide the cutoff marks of this BITSAT exam.

Eligibility Criteria for BITSAT 2017

  • Candidates must have completed HSC(10+2) Examination from a recognized university to apply for the examination.
  • Applicants must have scored at least 75% aggregate in PCM (Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics) or PCB (Physics, Chemistry and Biology) subjects and at least 60% of aggregate in all subjects individually to appear for the examination.
  • Applicants who are appearing 12th in 2017 and who have passed 12th in 2016 can apply for the examination.
  • Students who obtain first rank in any central or state level board exam in India held in the year 2016 will be given direct admission to the programme of their choice, irrespective of their BITSAT-2016 score subject to fulfilling the eligibility criteria.

BITSAT 2017 APPLICATION FORM

The application form has been made available only in the online mode. Candidates have to register online by entering their name, gender, date of birth and Email ID. After registering successfully applicants have to follow the steps mentioned below to apply for BITSAT 2017:

  • Applicants have to enter their personal details, academic details and choose the Test Centre where they want to appear for the test.
  • After entering the required details, candidates have to upload scanned images of photograph and signature.
  • Candidates have to pay the requisite fee mentioned below through online payment mode or through e-Challan payable at any branch of ICICI Bank.

After completing all the required steps, the filled in application form will be generated by the system. Candidates must download, take a print out of the form and keep it in a safe place for future reference.
